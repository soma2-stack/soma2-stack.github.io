# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 18, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Cole Rocherfort ("Developer," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with **School of the Dead** (the "Game"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
Currently, School of the Dead does not require account creation or personal information to play. We do not intentionally collect personal data such as your name, email address, or payment information through the Game itself.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
Depending on the platform or distribution method (e.g., Unity builds, itch.io, or similar), certain technical data may be collected automatically, including:
- Device type and operating system
- Game version and crash/error logs
- Gameplay session duration

This data, if collected, is used solely for debugging and improving the Game.

### 1.3 GitHub Repository
If you interact with this repository (e.g., open issues, submit pull requests, or fork the project), GitHub will collect your GitHub username and related activity. Please review [GitHub's Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement) for details.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used only to:
- Fix bugs and improve game performance
- Understand how the Game is being used
- Respond to user feedback or bug reports

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

School of the Dead is built using the Unity game engine. Unity may collect certain analytics data depending on build settings. Please review [Unity's Privacy Policy](https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy) for more information.

---

## 4. Data Retention

We retain any collected technical data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

School of the Dead is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. To exercise these rights, please contact us via GitHub.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## 8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please open an issue on this repository or contact the Developer through GitHub: [soma2-stack](https://github.com/soma2-stack).
